James And Jimmy

28 February, 2020 “Water Coaster”, a project by Barbara Materia and James was selected as the 2nd placed High Commendation award for the 2020 Brookes Street Jetty Competition.

05 October, 2019 “Double Trouble”, Jimmy’s reviews Edition Office’s Hawthorn House, is published in PLAT 8.0: Simplicity.

04 October, 2019 A Certain Kind of Life, opens at The Poetics of Reason, the 2019 Lisbon Architecture Triennale.

15 September, 2019 Abigail Chang, Francesco Marullo, Agata Siemionow and Jimmy have been awarded a grant by the Graham Foundation for Advanced Studies in the Fine Arts as representatives of UIC for the upcoming exhibition A Certain Kind of Life.

08 April, 2019 A Big Salad, a project by Jamie Goldsborough, Juan Suarez, Andrew Jennings and James was selected as a finalist for the 2019 Ragdale Ring.

15 February, 2019 A Certain Kind of Life, an exhibition by Abigail Chang, Francesco Marullo, Agata Siemionow and Jimmy has been chosen to participate as an Associated Project at 2019 Lisbon Triennale.

17 October, 2018 James’ article “Where You Always Are” is published in Flat Out #3.

11 October, 2018 Representing the University of Illinois at Chicago, the team of Jamie Goldsborough, Julian Gonzalez, Anjelica Hope Perez, Isabel Reford, William Stauffer,Angeliki Sykiotis, Ismael Vela, Thomas Kelly, Ryan Palider and Jimmy contributed to Ando and Le Corbusier: Masters of Architecture for the inaugural exhibition at Wrightwood 659.

06 October, 2018 Jimmy is to present Display Rooms as part of the 2018 ACSA Fall Conference Play with the Rules: CLUE (-d in or out).

06 April, 2018 Jimmy’s article “Spectators of Self-Deception” is published in Pidgin #23.

21 September, 2017 The architectural journal, Fresh Meat IX: Fake Fiction, co-edited by Jimmy launches at Volume Gallery in Chicago.

03 August, 2017 James is the recipient of the 2017 Schiff Foundation Critical Architectural Writing Fellowship.

01 December, 2016 A House for One is complete.

26 July, 2016 Jimmy launches the Australian premier of Bêka and Lemoine’s Barbicania, moderating a discussion with Clare Cousins (Clare Cousins Architects), Mark Raggatt (Ashton Raggatt McDougall), Louise Mackenzie (Cinecity).

23 June, 2016 James and Millie Anderson’s Karaoke Room for Tokyo Tina opens.

25 July, 2015 Jimmy launches the Australian premier of Angel Borrego Cubero’s The Competition with Linus Gruszewski. The panel includes Andrew Mackenzie, Annette Pitman, Gina Levenspiel, Kerstin Thompson, and Angel Borrego Cubero.

05 November, 2014 Jimmy presents “Anglophiles and Austericans” as part of MPavilion’s lunchtime dip into The Australian Ugliness.

30 October, 2014 James launches “Psychogeographical: A Melbourne dérive” with Linus Gruszewski as part of MPavilion.

28 October, 2014 James moderates “Distraction and Recognition” discussing the film Cathedrals of Culture with artist Callum Morton, architects Aaron Roberts and Kristin Green, and visual artist Marnie Edmiston.
