James And Jimmy

A Model for Louis and Tadao


Exhibition
Thomas Kelley, Ryan Palider, Angeliki Sykiotis, William Stauffer, Isabel Reford, Jamie Goldsborough, Julian Gonzalez, Anjelica Hope Perez, Ismael Vela and Jimmy
Wrightwood 659
2018
Chicago, USA

Seeking to exacerbate distinctions between two architectural endeavours, this model investigates difference through similarity. Made for Ando and Le Corbusier: Masters of Architecture as the inaugural exhibition at Wrightwood 659, the model reformulates Tadao Ando’s Fort Worth Modern Art Museum, flattening predecessors and descendants into an architecture of friendship.
