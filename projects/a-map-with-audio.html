James And Jimmy

A Map with Audio


Audio-Exhibition
Linus Gruszewski and James
MPavilion
2014
Melbourne, Australia

A self-guided audio exhibition within the Queen Victoria Gardens, “Psychogeographical: A Melbourne dérive” is a trip on which to discover the unknown stories of familiar places. Taking place during MPavilion’s 2014 summer, the exhibition constructs a territory that utilizes synchronous narratives to punctuate the experience of objects, events and encounters with memory, reason and humour.
