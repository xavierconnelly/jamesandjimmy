James And Jimmy

A Certain Kind Of Life


Exhibition
Abigail Chang, Agata Siemionow, Francesco Marullo, Jimmy
Lisbon Architecture Triennale
2019
Lisbon, Portugal

An exhibition that explores the broadened contemporary asceticism within its spatial lineage, “A Certain Kind of Life” exposes the typology of the charterhouse as an architecture of absolute rationality. Taking place at the Cartuxa de Laveiras as parts of the 2019 Lisbon Triennale , interventions across the site included the installation of a cell—the paradigmatic evocation of asceticism; research material detailing the typological repetition and composition of several monasteries; projects for contemporary living; and a series of talks discussing ritual as rationalism and its resultant architectural form.
