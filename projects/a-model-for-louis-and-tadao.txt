        <h1>A Model for Louis and Tadao</h1>
        <br>
        <h4>
          Exhibition <br><hr>

        </h4>

      <span id="project-text">
        <p>
          Seeking to exacerbate distinctions between two architectural endeavours, this model investigates difference through similarity. Made for <a href="https://wrightwood659.org/exhibitions/ando-and-le-corbusier-masters-of-architecture">Ando and Le Corbusier: Masters of Architecture</a>
          as the inaugural exhibition at Wrightwood 659, the model reformulates Tadao Ando’s Fort Worth Modern Art Museum, flattening predecessors and descendants into an architecture of friendship.
